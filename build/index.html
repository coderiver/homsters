About Homsters

HOMSTERS IS A REAL ESTATE PROMOTION AND TECHNOLOGY COMPANY. WE PROVIDE REAL ESTATE SELLERS WITH MARKETPLACES AND VALUE ADDED SERVICES SPECIFICALLY DESIGNED TO PERFORM ON EMERGING MARKETS.

Our real estate choices are some of the most important and expensive ones in life. At the same time real estate providers have trouble adapting to the digital era and spend billions on advertising, and yet – the customer experience they create does not fit the expectations of the new age buyers. This creates enormous inefficiencies, especially on emerging markets. We believe that there has to be a better way!

Our passion is creating marketplaces, which:

  • – will help developers and brokers transition into the digital era and efficiently compete for customers with the use of performance marketing.
  • – provide buyers with relevant, comparable, transparent information about their property choices

Our Markets

Homsters Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is the world's largest landlocked country by land area and the ninth largest country in the world. One of the highest GDPs among CIS countries and a competitive economy, which drives a growth of a massive, but young real estate sector. 70% of the real estate market volume is generated by the primary real estate sector.

The international expansion of Homsters began in Kazakhstan, where in September 2015 the online-marketplace launched. Our goal is to bring all the benefits of international best practices, adapted to the use of the Kazakhstani real estate market.

Homsters Vietnam

Vietnam with an estimated 90.5 million inhabitants as of 2014, it is the world's 13th-most-populous country, and the eighth-most-populous Asian country. With a $2073 GDP per capita in 2014 and IMF forecast for 2020 6% p.a. growth it is also one of the fastest growing emerging markets. The GDP growth will drive real estate market size dramatically.

The Vietnamese real estate market is already very competitive and has opened up to foreign investments in the summer of 2015. Combined with all the demographical factors and the growing internet penetration, we believe, this will trigger the growth of transaction volume, prices and further increase of competition among real estate sellers, making it an ideal market for the launch of a performance driven online-marketplace.
